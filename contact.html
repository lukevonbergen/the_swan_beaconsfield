Getting Here

By Car

We're located on London End in the heart of Beaconsfield. Limited parking is available on nearby streets, with a public car park just 2 minutes walk away on Warwick Road.

By Train

Beaconsfield station is a 5-minute walk from The Swan. Regular services run from London Marylebone (25 minutes) and Birmingham (1 hour).

By Bus

Several local bus routes stop near The Swan, connecting to High Wycombe, Slough, and surrounding villages.

Nearby Landmarks

  • Beaconsfield Old Town - 1 minute walk
  • Beaconsfield Station - 5 minute walk
  • The National Film and Television School - 10 minute walk
  • Burnham Beeches - 15 minute drive
  • Jordans Quaker Meeting House - 10 minute drive

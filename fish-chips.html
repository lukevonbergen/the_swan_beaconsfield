Our Signature Fish & Chips

Our classic Fish & Chips is a celebration of British tradition, featuring the freshest cod sourced daily from sustainable fisheries. Each piece is carefully hand-battered in our signature beer batter made with local ale, creating that perfect golden crispy exterior while keeping the fish moist and flaky inside.

Served alongside our famous hand-cut chips, made from locally grown potatoes and cooked in beef dripping for that authentic pub taste. Accompanied by traditional mushy peas and a choice of tartare sauce or malt vinegar.

What Makes It Special

  • Fresh North Sea cod, delivered daily
  • Beer batter made with Chiltern Brewery ale
  • Hand-cut chips from local farms
  • Traditional mushy peas
  • House-made tartare sauce
  • Cooked to order for perfect freshness

Allergen Information

Contains: Fish, Gluten (wheat flour), Eggs, Sulphites. Cooked in oil that may also contain traces of other allergens. Please inform staff of any allergies.

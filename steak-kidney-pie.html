Steak & Kidney Pie

£16.95

Traditional Steak & Kidney Pie

A true British classic, our Steak & Kidney Pie is made the traditional way with tender chunks of prime British beef and lamb's kidneys, slow-cooked for hours in a rich, savory gravy infused with fresh herbs and a splash of local ale.

Encased in our house-made buttery shortcrust pastry and topped with golden puff pastry, this hearty dish is served piping hot with your choice of creamy mashed potatoes or hand-cut chips, seasonal vegetables, and our signature gravy on the side.

What Makes It Special

  • Prime British beef, sourced locally
  • Traditional lamb's kidneys for authentic flavor
  • Slow-cooked for 4+ hours
  • House-made pastry, rolled fresh daily
  • Rich gravy with local ale
  • Served with seasonal vegetables

Allergen Information

Contains: Gluten (wheat flour), Eggs, Milk. May contain traces of nuts. Please inform staff of any allergies.
